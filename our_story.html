Our Story

Shaw + Scott was founded on the belief that there has to be a better way to provide exceptional marketing services and build an agency designed for the modern world where diversity, flexibility, partnership and innovation thrive.

Since our founding in 2009, Shaw + Scott has grown from two founders and a couple contractors to 85+ people across the globe due to our reputation for delivering outstanding quality, insight, value and experiences.

We are an award-winning, women/LGBT founded and led agency with an unwavering commitment to equality and opportunity. We achieve this by crafting scenarios for both our team and clients that empower flexibility and foster success. We want everyone who is part of the Shaw + Scott family to love what they do, love their job and love their life. That’s what we call being Shaw + Scottish.

With teams in Seattle, San Francisco, Los Angeles, Chicago, New York, Vancouver and London, we are just getting started and are continually expanding our services and offerings that will both revolutionize and harmonize your digital marketing eco-system.

After all, we want to be the agency you’ll love.
